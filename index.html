What is Multi-Level Queue Scheduling?

Multi-Level Queue Scheduling divides processes into multiple queues based on priority or type. Each queue has its own scheduling rules. The CPU executes higher priority queues first.

Queues

  • High Priority Queue: System processes
  • Medium Priority Queue: Interactive processes
  • Low Priority Queue: Batch processes
